About us

Inspiring everyone, helping who we can.

Since our inception in 2014, Breaking Beats has been using arts to help people live better lives. We’ve seen a range of different issues in and around different Doncaster communities and we wanted to affect change. A decade later, there is still a lack of skills, rampant poverty and a shortage of inspiration.

We still have work to do.
